About Regan O'Brien

UX Designer

I've studied humans in a lot of different environments and am obsessed with human behavior, learning, and behavior change.

I've studied humans in many different settings.
As a Personal Trainer I learned process, problem solving, motivation, adaptation, effective communication.
In the rehab realm, I learned about human behavior change and effective communication.
In food service and event planning, I learned persuasion, adapting on the fly, multi-tasking, organization, communicating to populations of varied backgrounds.
Working in senior living, I learned patience, and how to interpret what people say and drill down to what they're actually saying and needing.
Now, I get to take all that I've learned and use it to it's utmost potential in this fascinating, wide-reaching medium of UX Research, and interaction between humans and machines.
